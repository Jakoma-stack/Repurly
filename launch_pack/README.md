# Repurly Final Launch Pack

This pack gives you the fastest practical path to launch, sell, and onboard customers under the Repurly brand while using the stronger Repurly codebase underneath.

## What is inside

### 1) app_integration/
Ready-to-drop files for a Flask/Jinja app structure:
- `templates/landing.html` - public homepage with hero, problem, workflow, pricing preview, testimonials, FAQ, and CTA
- `templates/pricing.html` - simple pricing page for pilots, growth, and agency offers
- `templates/book_demo.html` - demo booking / contact page
- `examples/routes_example.py` - example Flask routes to wire the pages into your app

### 2) docs/
Execution and sales materials:
- `sales_playbook.md` - positioning, ICP, pricing, and close strategy
- `offer_page_copy.md` - exact service offer page copy
- `pricing_page_copy.md` - exact pricing page copy
- `homepage_copy.md` - homepage copy in plain text form
- `demo_script.md` - 10-minute demo flow and close script
- `outbound_messages.md` - outreach templates for agencies, founders, and follow-ups
- `30_day_revenue_plan.md` - idiot-proof 30-day daily execution plan
- `repurly_ready_fixes.md` - critical fixes before pushing sales hard
- `brand_notes.md` - brand naming and positioning notes

### 3) sales_assets/
Previously generated founder docs and PDFs bundled into one place.

## Fastest use order
1. Put `app_integration/templates/landing.html` live on `repurly.org`
2. Add your Calendly link in the CTA buttons
3. Add routes from `app_integration/examples/routes_example.py`
4. Read `docs/repurly_ready_fixes.md` and fix those items first
5. Use `docs/outbound_messages.md` and `docs/demo_script.md` to book and close your first pilots
6. Follow `docs/30_day_revenue_plan.md` daily

## Important positioning rule
Do not sell Repurly as an AI writing tool.
Sell it as a content operations system that helps businesses create once, approve fast, and publish consistently.
