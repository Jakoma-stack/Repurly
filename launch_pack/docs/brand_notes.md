# Repurly brand notes

## Public brand
Use **Repurly** publicly because it matches the domains you already bought and is cleaner than PostEngine.

## Recommended lockup
- Product: **Repurly**
- Endorsement: **Built by Jakoma**
- Short line: **Create once. Approve fast. Publish with confidence.**

## Positioning
Repurly is not "an AI content generator". Repurly is a content operations system for agencies and founder-led B2B teams that need one place to create, review, approve, schedule, and publish content reliably.

## Website structure
- `repurly.org` -> product site and demo CTA
- Jakoma site -> parent company, trust signal, product/app listing, and cross-link
