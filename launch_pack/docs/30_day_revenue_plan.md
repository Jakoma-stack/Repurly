# Repurly 30-Day Revenue Plan

## Non-negotiable rule
Focus on one product: Repurly as the brand, Repurly as the engine underneath.
Do not split energy between two separate products.

## Daily scorecard
Every working day:
- Send 20 targeted outreach messages
- Start 2 to 4 conversations
- Book 1 to 2 demos
- Follow up every open lead
- Log objections and product friction

## Week 1 - Get ready to sell
### Goals
- Landing page live
- Demo workspace ready
- Calendly link ready
- 100-prospect list built

### Actions
- Finalise homepage and pricing page
- Add CTA links
- Build prospect list of agencies, founders, and consultants
- Start outreach by day 3

### Outcome target
5 to 10 demos booked

## Week 2 - Get first money in
### Goals
- Run demos daily
- Pitch paid pilot every time
- Improve based on objections

### Outcome target
1 to 3 paying customers
1k to 5k revenue

## Week 3 - Tighten and raise price
### Goals
- Remove onboarding friction
- Increase confidence in offer
- Raise pricing for new leads if early demos go well

### Outcome target
3k to 8k MRR plus setup revenue

## Week 4 - Push bigger deals
### Goals
- Target agencies harder
- Sell multi-brand and higher-touch setups
- Use proof and testimonials

### Outcome target
5k to 15k MRR plus setup revenue

## Revenue logic
20k per month is realistic if you sell service-assisted software.
Examples:
- 5 clients at 4k per month
- 10 clients at 2k per month
- 8 clients at 1k per month plus setup fees and a few larger accounts

## What not to do
- Do not launch both brands separately
- Do not wait for perfect product polish
- Do not compete as a cheap AI writing tool
- Do not rely on SEO first
- Do not price too low
