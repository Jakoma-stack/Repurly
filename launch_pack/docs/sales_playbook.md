# Repurly Sales Playbook

## Positioning
Do not sell AI generation.
Sell workflow reliability.

## Core promise
Create once. Approve fast. Publish everywhere.

## Best buyers
- Small agencies with multiple clients
- Founder-led B2B businesses
- Consultants and operators running personal brand content
- Small marketing teams with approval bottlenecks

## Fastest monetisation route
Sell a managed pilot or setup-first offer.
Not pure low-ticket self-serve SaaS.

## Offer structure
### Starter pilot
- £750 to £1,500 setup
- £299 to £599 per month

### Growth
- £2,000 to £5,000 setup
- £750 to £2,000 per month

### Agency
- 1,000 to 3,000+ per month depending on brands and support
- Consider setup fees for larger implementations

## Sales angle
Your buyer does not want an AI content toy.
They want:
- fewer approval bottlenecks
- less admin
- fewer missed posts
- better visibility
- a repeatable process

## Demo objective
Get them to say: "Yes, this would remove a lot of friction for us."

## Close objective
Move them into a paid pilot or setup engagement.

## Price confidence line
"You are not just paying for access to software. You are paying for a content workflow that actually gets used."
