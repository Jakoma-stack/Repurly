# Jakoma integration recommendation

## Recommendation
Keep Repurly as its own product brand and site, then present it on the Jakoma site as a featured product or app.

## Why
- A standalone brand converts better for SaaS buyers because it looks like a product, not a services page.
- Jakoma still adds trust as the builder and operator.
- You keep the option to sell, spin out, or raise the profile of Repurly later.

## Best positioning
Use one of these labels:
- Repurly by Jakoma
- Built by Jakoma
- A Jakoma product

## Best website structure
- repurly.org = main product site and main conversion path
- jakoma.org/products or jakoma.org/apps = short product overview with a link through to repurly.org

## Do not
- hide Repurly entirely inside the Jakoma site navigation as if it is just one services subpage
- lead with Jakoma over Repurly on the product homepage

## Commercial logic
This gives you trust from Jakoma while keeping a cleaner SaaS brand and better perceived product value.
