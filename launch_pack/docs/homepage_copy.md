# Repurly homepage copy

## Positioning
Repurly is a content operations platform for agencies, consultants, and founder-led teams. It is built by Jakoma and should be positioned as the operational layer behind consistent publishing, approvals, and multi-brand control.

## Hero
**Create once. Approve fast. Publish with confidence.**

Repurly gives your team one clean workflow to plan, review, approve, schedule, and publish content across brands without scattered docs, missed handoffs, or messy approval chains.

**Primary CTA:** Book a demo  
**Secondary CTA:** See how it works

## Proof strip
- Multi-brand workflow
- Approval control
- Publishing visibility

## Problem section
**Content should not depend on Slack threads, spreadsheets, and memory.**

Most teams do not have a content problem. They have an operations problem. Drafts live in one place, feedback lives in another, approvals get delayed, and publishing becomes inconsistent.

## Solution section
**Fix the workflow, not just the writing.**

Repurly brings planning, review, approvals, scheduling, and publishing into one accountable flow so your team can move faster with fewer mistakes.

## Differentiation
**Not another AI content tool.**

Most tools help you generate content. Repurly helps you operate content. That is what makes it easier to deliver work consistently for clients and across brands.

## Credibility
**Built by Jakoma.**

Position Repurly as a specialist product from Jakoma, not as a side project. On the site, use the line: *Repurly by Jakoma* or *Built by Jakoma*.

## Final CTA
**Ready to make content ops less chaotic?**

Book a demo and see the workflow live.
