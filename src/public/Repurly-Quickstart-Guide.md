# Repurly Quickstart Guide

## 1. Create your workspace
Invite your team, confirm billing, and create your first brand.

## 2. Connect LinkedIn
Use the guided connect flow. Select a member profile or an eligible page once access is discovered.

## 3. Upload assets
Upload source images or video to your workspace storage.

## 4. Draft content
Create a LinkedIn post, choose the target profile or company page, and route it for approval before scheduling.

## 5. Approve and schedule
Move approved posts into the publish queue with a date and time.

## 6. Monitor delivery
Use the publishing queue and audit trail to track retries, failures, and successful publishes.
