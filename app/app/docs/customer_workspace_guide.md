# Customer Workspace Guide

## Best-practice content flow

1. Complete **Brands** with tone, audience and CTA.
2. Upload logos and visual assets in **Assets**.
3. Open **Content & schedule** and start with the **AI campaign planner**.
4. Choose your cadence:
   - Daily for 1 week
   - Weekdays for 2 weeks
   - 3 times per week
   - Weekly
   - Custom draft count
5. Choose your publish timing:
   - Same time every post
   - Stagger through the day
   - Random workday times
6. Generate drafts, then edit any copy you want before review or scheduling.
7. Submit for review if you want a check before publishing, or schedule directly when the content is ready.

## Format guide

- **Text**: no asset selected
- **Single image**: exactly one asset selected
- **Carousel**: two or more assets selected

## AI guidance

The strongest outputs happen when your brand profile is complete and your brief is specific.
Use a campaign-level brief like:
- "Launch our AI governance offer to B2B marketers"
- "Generate onboarding tips for operations leaders"
- "Create proof-driven posts about client delivery results"
