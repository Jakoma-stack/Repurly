FAQ

Questions buyers usually ask before paying.

What is Repurly best for?

Repurly is best for agencies managing recurring LinkedIn content for multiple client brands and wanting a more controlled workflow for onboarding, approvals, scheduling, and supported publishing.

What can go live today?

LinkedIn text posts and LinkedIn single-image posts can be supported for live publishing when the relevant brand credentials are configured correctly.

Does the product include onboarding and billing?

Yes. The latest build includes customer signup, checkout support, password setup, guided onboarding, billing access, workspace settings, and team-aware plan structure.

Are carousels fully supported for live posting?

Not yet. Carousel planning and validation may exist inside the workflow, but live LinkedIn carousel publishing should not be sold as fully supported until that path is completed and validated.
