# Phase 0 - Locked commercial wedge

## 90-day ICP

**Our first customer is small agencies managing LinkedIn content for multiple client brands.**

## Full ICP definition

We are building first for small marketing, content, or social media agencies that manage recurring LinkedIn content for multiple client brands and need a safer, faster workflow for generating, approving, scheduling, and publishing posts.

## What this customer looks like

- 2-15 client brands
- small internal team or founder-led delivery model
- repeat monthly content workflow
- client review and approval steps
- manual coordination through spreadsheets, docs, chat, email, or generic scheduling tools

## Core problem

They are not mainly struggling with how to write a caption. They are struggling with:

- slow approvals
- messy client coordination
- repeated manual setup per client
- switching between brands/accounts
- risk of posting from the wrong account
- inconsistent delivery process
- too much time spent turning briefs into scheduled content

## What they want

- a repeatable monthly content workflow
- faster draft-to-approval turnaround
- clearer visibility on what is ready, approved, and scheduled
- lower publishing risk
- less operational chaos
- the ability to handle more client brands without adding as much overhead

## Locked statements

- **ICP:** small agencies managing LinkedIn content for multiple client brands
- **Platform:** LinkedIn only until paid pilots succeed
- **Promise:** approve and publish a month of client LinkedIn content safely in one workflow
- **Offer:** founding agency launch offer with setup fee plus monthly recurring fee

## Decision filter

Prioritize only work that helps small agencies managing LinkedIn content for multiple client brands generate, approve, schedule, or publish content more safely and efficiently.
