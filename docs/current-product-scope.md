# Current product scope

## Commercial position
Repurly is a premium LinkedIn-first content operations product for agencies and B2B teams.

## Core product surfaces
- marketing site and pricing
- sign-in and sign-up
- overview/dashboard
- composer and AI drafting
- brands
- calendar and queue
- channels and target selection
- activity and reliability
- notifications
- reports
- billing
- settings, operator controls, invites, and members
- engagement and lightweight leads

## What makes the product credible
- approvals and workflow control
- clearer target selection before publishing
- reporting and recovery views
- paid-product posture rather than free-scheduler posture

## Deliberate non-goals right now
- broad all-network social suite positioning
- full CRM scope
- fully mature enterprise listening suite
