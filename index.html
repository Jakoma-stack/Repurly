How it works

A four-step workflow your team can actually keep using.

Repurly is designed as an operating flow, not just a prompt box. That matters because consistent publishing depends on sequence, clarity and approval control.

1. Plan content

Map posts to content themes, client priorities and campaign intent before drafting begins.

2. Draft faster

Generate stronger first drafts inside a structured workflow instead of starting from a blank page every time.

3. Approve clearly

Review content, capture changes and keep sign-off visible before anything is queued.

4. Publish with confidence

Move approved posts into a cleaner publishing flow with fewer manual mistakes.
