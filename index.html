How it works

A workflow designed for real agency delivery, from first signup to scheduled post.

Repurly is not just a drafting tool. It is a sequence that starts with customer setup and ends with cleaner scheduling and publishing support.

1. Start and activate

Customers enter through a public signup and checkout flow, then activate their account and workspace.

2. Set up brands

Add brands, upload assets and prepare the account structure needed for repeatable client delivery.

3. Draft and review

Create content, attach assets, choose format and move posts through review or direct scheduling.

4. Schedule and oversee

Schedule ready posts and keep ops-level visibility available for review, retries and audit tracking.

Supported publishing today

  • LinkedIn text posts
  • LinkedIn single-image posts
  • Customer-managed draft and schedule flow
  • Ops review and publish retry support

Important scope note

Repurly is intentionally LinkedIn-first. That keeps the workflow sharper, easier to trust and easier to monetise with the right agencies.

Carousel workflows can be prepared inside the system, but live carousel publishing should not be presented as fully supported yet.
