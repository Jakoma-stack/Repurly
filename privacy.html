Repurly privacy notice

This notice explains how Repurly handles personal data submitted through this website and the demo access form. Repurly is built and operated by Jakoma.

Contact: support@repurly.org is the main contact point for privacy questions, access requests and beta-enquiry follow-up.

1. Who is responsible for your data

Data controller: Repurly
Built and operated by: Jakoma
Contact email: support@repurly.org

2. What data we may collect

  • Contact details you provide in the beta form or by email, such as your name, email address, company name and message content.
  • Basic usage and technical data recorded through standard server logs, such as IP address, browser type, device information and request details.
  • Referral and attribution data carried through URL parameters, such as UTM source, medium, campaign and content values.

3. Why we use this data

  • To operate, secure and improve the website and beta environment.
  • To review beta requests, respond to support enquiries and contact prospective customers.
  • To understand which website links and campaigns are driving traffic and signups.
  • To maintain audit, operational and security records where reasonably necessary.

4. Lawful bases

Depending on the situation, we may rely on:

  • Legitimate interests for operating, securing and improving Repurly and responding to business enquiries.
  • Steps prior to entering a contract where you request access, pricing or onboarding information.
  • Consent where consent is required, for example if non-essential cookies or opt-in marketing are introduced later.

5. Cookies and similar technologies

The marketing site is intended to run without non-essential cookies or advertising trackers by default. If analytics, remarketing tools or embedded third-party services are added later, this notice and any required consent flow should be updated before those tools are activated.

6. Sharing data

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with service providers that help us host, operate, secure or support Repurly where that is reasonably necessary and subject to appropriate safeguards.

7. International transfers

If suppliers or infrastructure providers process data outside your home jurisdiction, we aim to use appropriate safeguards where required, such as adequacy decisions or standard contractual clauses.

8. Retention

We keep personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for support, onboarding, security, legal compliance and service improvement.

9. Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to request access, correction, erasure, restriction, objection and, in some cases, portability. You may also have the right to complain to a relevant supervisory authority.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@repurly.org.

11. Updates

This notice may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the website, beta workflow or legal obligations. The latest version will always be published here.
